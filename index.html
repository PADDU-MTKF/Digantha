ABOUT DIGANTHA

Digantha, an essence of unrivalled expertise 2023, is an inter collegiate students talent hunt which aims to provide opportunities to students of undergraduate courses. It is an effective blend of competitions All Rounder, Variety, Street Play, Face Painting, Marketing, HR, Lab Event, Documentary, Technical and model making. The competition carry attractive prizes, overall championship trophy and participation certificates.

ABOUT THE COLLEGE

Govinda Dasa College, founded in the year 1967 and affiliated to Mangalore University offers bachelor degree courses in Arts, Commerce, Business Administration, Science and Computer Application for both girls and boys. It is accredited by NAAC with A Grade (CGPA 3.14). The Hindu Vidyadayinee sangha (R.), which manages the college and other sister institutions, was founded on 30-11-1916, with the startup of Iddya Vidyadayinee school in the precincts of Shri Iddya Mahalingeshwara Temple, by the visionary teachers and well wishers.
