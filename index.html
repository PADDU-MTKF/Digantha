ABOUT DIGANTHA 2025

Digantha, a new horizon to spread your wings is an inter-collegiate student's genius hunt organized by the PG and UG students of GDC, which aims to provide opportunities to students of undergraduate courses. It is an effective blend of competitions comprising All Rounder, Group Song, Dance, Street Play, Face Painting, Marketing, HR, Best Management Team, Finance, Lab Events, Documentary, Technical and Poster Making. The competition offers enticing prizes, an overall championship trophy and participation certificates to all participants.

RULES:-


  • The competition is open exclusively to undergraduate students.
  • Participants must compulsorily carry their bonafide certificate from their college with valid ID cards.
  • Each college is permitted to send a team comprising of only 40 students.
  • The team must promptly report on the same day at the registration desk by 8:30 A.M.
  • The contestants should refer to all the rules stated in both the brochure as well as in the official website of "The Digantha".
  • Any display of vulgarity or indecency during the event will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Use of fire or explosives is strictly prohibited.
  • The decisions of the judges shall be final.
  • Registration should be done before 3rd April 2025.
  • Online Registration closes on 3rd April 2025 at 12:30AM.

FINEARTS RULES:-


  • Participants who are participating in finearts events are not allowed to participate in any other events.
  • Group participants are not allowed to participate in individual events.
  • Each team consists not more than 25 members.
  • Group & individual events will be conducted simultaneously.


ABOUT THE COLLEGE

Govinda Dasa College, eshtablished in the year 1967 and affiliated to Mangalore University offers Bachelor Degree courses in Arts, Commerce, Business Administration, Science and Computer Application and also Post Graduation courses in Commerce & Science for both girls and boys. Our college is accredited and recognized by NAAC with ‘A’ Grade (CGPA 3.14). The Hindu Vidyadayinee Sangha (R.), which manages the college and other sister institutions established in the year 1916, with the startup of Iddya Vidyadayinee School in the precincts of Shri Iddya Mahalingeshwara Temple, by the visionary teachers and well wishers. Our college has been an accomplished and estimable ,promoted by great visionaries .It has a team of highly qualified and research oriented faculty supported by a green campus with state of the art infrastructure. Govinda Dasa College stands out as an institution of excellence in training individuals for outstanding caliber.
