    • RULES:-

    • The competition is open exclusively to undergraduate students.
    • Participants must compulsorily carry their bonafide certificate from their college with valid ID cards.
    • Each college is permitted to send a team comprising of only 40 students.
    • The team must promptly report on the same day at the registration desk by 8:30 A.M.
    • The contestants should refer to all the rules stated in both the brochure as well as in the official website of "The Digantha".
    • Any display of vulgarity or indecency during the event will result in immediate disqualification.
    • Use of fire or explosives is strictly prohibited.
    • The decisions of the judges shall be final.
    • Registration should be done before 9th May 2024.

    FINEARTS RULES:-


    • Participants who are participating in finearts events are not allowed to participate in any other events.
    • Group participants are not allowed to participate in individual events.
    • Each team consists not more than 18 members.
    • Group & individual events will be conducted simultaneously.
