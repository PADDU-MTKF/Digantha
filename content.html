    • RULES:-


    • The competition is open exclusively to undergraduate students.
    • The judges' decisions are final and binding.
    • Participants must carry their bonafide certificate and valid ID cards for verification.
    • Each college is allowed to send a team consisting of a maximum of 40 students.
    • Vulgarity, indecency, fire, and explosives are strictly prohibited. Participants must complete registration before May 31, 2023. On the day of the event, participants must register at the registration desk by 8:30 am. Additionally, participants are required to refer to all the rules stated in both the brochure and the official website of "The Digantha" for comprehensive information and guidelines.
    • Participants engaged in Finearts events are not permitted to participate in any other events.
    • Group participants are restricted from participating in individual events.
    • Group and individual events will be conducted simultaneously, offering participants a variety of options.
    • Participants are expected to adhere to these guidelines to ensure fairness and equal opportunity for all.
